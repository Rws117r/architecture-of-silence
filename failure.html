CRITICAL FRACTURE ANALYSIS

Subject: The Concrete Mask (Pressure Vessel Breach)

The fracture of the concrete mask is not a failure of the skeletal structure itself, but a predictable outcome of the Mechanical Clinch. The architecture functions as a reactive system where the internal framework (The Ribs - Say It Like It Holds) over-reinforces for control to absolute saturation.

The mask serves as a Performative Pressure Vessel. It is designed to contain the internal storm of structural stress, presenting outward as a smooth, finished object of silence. However, as the internal clinch intensifies, the outward thrust exceeds the tensile capacity of the shell.

Analysis of Phase D

This stress event marks the moment where "having it under control" is destroyed by the actual effort of trying to control it. The architecture survives the breach; it is only the calcified performance (The Mask) that is lost.

"The 'Fracture' is a recorded failure of the mask to container internal reinforcement stresses. It bridges the gap between the static blueprint of the dome and the kinetic reality of the failed performance."
